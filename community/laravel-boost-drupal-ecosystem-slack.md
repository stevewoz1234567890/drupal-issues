# Laravel Boost vs Drupal — community Slack thread

**Context:** Informal Drupal Slack discussion. Names and handles as in the original messages.

## Thread (paraphrased)

- **Thread opener:** After trying **Laravel Boost**, they understand why people keep asking for a **Drupal equivalent**—Boost is **strong** at what it does.
- **Angie Byron (webchick):** Has not tried Boost yet; asks **what specifically** people value about it.
- **Ronald te Brake (ronaldtebrake):** **Agrees** with the sentiment. Work in the Drupal ecosystem was **inspired by Laravel Boost** (including from an earlier blog post). He **opened** [AI Initiative issue #3541110](https://www.drupal.org/project/ai_initiative/issues/3541110) and, from that line of thinking, began **[Surge](https://www.drupal.org/project/surge)**. For a while the space felt **very fragmented**; **more puzzle pieces are aligning** now. **[AI Best Practices for Drupal](https://www.drupal.org/project/ai_best_practices)** is highlighted as a **natural hub** to converge on something **in the same spirit** as what Boost achieves for Laravel.
- **Another participant:** They had **used Surge** before **using Boost**; only tried Boost **recently**. They were at **Laracon** when Boost was **announced** but had not been **building heavily in Laravel** until more recently—so their **ordering** of exposure (Surge first, Boost second) differs from others in the thread.
